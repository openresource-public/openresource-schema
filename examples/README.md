# Examples

Real lessons in the schema, to read alongside [SCHEMA.md](../SCHEMA.md).

| File | Subject | Key stage | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| [1066-norman-conquest.json](./1066-norman-conquest.json) | History | KS3 | Oak National Academy |
| [depositional-coastal-landforms.json](./depositional-coastal-landforms.json) | Geography | KS3 | Oak National Academy |

`1066-norman-conquest.json` is "The 1066 succession crisis" — 26 blocks across 4 sections, covering `content`, `question_choice`, `question_ordering`, `question_text_input`, and `primitive_glossary`. Imported from an Oak slide deck and structured by an agent.

`depositional-coastal-landforms.json` is "Depositional coastal landforms" — 33 blocks across 4 sections. A more diagram-heavy lesson that also exercises `primitive_layered` (×5), `primitive_flow`, `content_table`, and `question_labelling`.
